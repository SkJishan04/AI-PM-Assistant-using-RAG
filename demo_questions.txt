Demo questions for AI PM Assistant

Upload demo_document.txt, click "Index documents", then try asking these questions.

--- Simple fact lookup ---
1. What is the default notification lead time?
2. What are the customization options for notification lead time?
3. Who is the assignee for TASK-104?

--- Summarization across a section ---
4. What risks are mentioned in this PRD?
5. What were the main discussion points in the design review meeting?
6. What is out of scope for v1?

--- Multi-fact / requires connecting two parts of the doc ---
7. What action items came out of the design review, and who owns each one?
8. Which Jira tickets are blocked, and why?
9. What are the success metrics, and how do they relate to the goals?

--- Reasoning slightly beyond a single sentence ---
10. Why might the personalized lead-time suggestion feature be risky?
11. What engineering risk was raised around cross-device sync, and how was it resolved?

--- Should say "I don't know" (tests grounding, not hallucination) ---
12. What is the budget for this project?
13. Who is the engineering manager overseeing this team?
14. What programming language is the app built in?

Tip: questions 12-14 are intentionally not answerable from the document.
A good answer should say it doesn't know rather than guessing.
